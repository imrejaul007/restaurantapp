## Account Setup

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit RestaurantHub**: Go to [restauranthub.com/signup](https://restauranthub.com/signup)
2. **Select Restaurant Owner**: Choose "Restaurant Owner" as your account type
3. **Fill Basic Information**:
   - First and Last Name
   - Email Address (this will be your login)
   - Phone Number (for verification)
   - Strong Password (minimum 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character)

4. **Restaurant Information**:
   - Restaurant Name
   - Cuisine Type (select multiple if applicable)
   - Primary Location/City

5. **Accept Terms**: Review and accept our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
6. **Create Account**: Click "Create Account" to complete registration

### Email Verification

After creating your account:

1. **Check Your Email**: Look for a verification email from RestaurantHub
2. **Click Verification Link**: This will confirm your email address
3. **Login**: Return to the platform and log in with your credentials

> **Note**: Some features may be limited until email verification is complete.

## Restaurant Profile Creation

### Basic Information

Once logged in, complete your restaurant profile:

1. **Restaurant Details**:
   - Full restaurant name
   - Description/About section
   - Cuisine types (Indian, Chinese, Continental, etc.)
   - Operating hours
   - Contact information

2. **Location Information**:
   - Primary address
   - City, State, Pincode
   - Additional branch locations (if applicable)
   - GPS coordinates (auto-detected)

3. **Visual Assets**:
   - Restaurant logo (recommended: 300x300px, PNG/JPG)
   - Cover photo/banner (recommended: 1200x400px)
   - Interior/food photos (optional)

### Business Information

For full platform access, provide:

1. **Legal Information**:
   - Business registration number
   - GST number (if applicable)
   - FSSAI license number
   - PAN number

2. **Banking Details** (for vendor payments):
   - Bank account number
   - Bank name and branch
   - IFSC code
   - Account holder name

3. **Ownership Documents**:
   - Business license
   - FSSAI certificate
   - GST registration certificate

## Verification Process

### Document Upload

1. **Navigate to Verification**: Go to Profile → Verification
2. **Upload Documents**:
   - Business license (clear photo or PDF)
   - FSSAI certificate
   - GST registration (if applicable)
   - Bank account proof

3. **Document Requirements**:
   - Clear, readable images
   - Valid and current documents
   - Consistent business name across documents

### Verification Timeline

- **Initial Review**: 24-48 hours
- **Additional Info Request**: 1-2 business days
- **Final Approval**: 3-5 business days

### Verification Benefits

Once verified, you get:

## Posting Your First Job

### Job Creation Process

1. **Navigate to Jobs**: Click "Jobs" → "Create New Job"
2. **Basic Information**:
   - Job title (e.g., "Head Chef", "Waiter", "Kitchen Assistant")
   - Department/category
   - Job type (Full-time, Part-time, Contract)
   - Location (specific branch if multiple)

3. **Job Description**:
   - Detailed role description
   - Key responsibilities
   - Required qualifications
   - Preferred experience
   - Skills needed

4. **Requirements**:
   - Minimum education
   - Experience level (0-20 years)
   - Language requirements
   - Physical requirements (if applicable)
   - Certifications needed

5. **Compensation**:
   - Salary range (minimum and maximum)
   - Benefits offered
   - Additional perks
   - Performance incentives

6. **Application Settings**:
   - Application deadline
   - Required documents (resume, portfolio, etc.)
   - Interview process
   - Start date preference

### Job Posting Best Practices

#### Writing Effective Job Titles
- Be specific: "Experienced North Indian Chef" vs "Chef"
- Include level: "Senior Waiter", "Junior Sous Chef"
- Mention specialization: "Tandoor Chef", "Barista"

#### Crafting Compelling Descriptions
- Start with restaurant overview
- Clearly outline responsibilities
- Highlight growth opportunities
- Mention work culture and benefits
- Use bullet points for readability

#### Setting Competitive Compensation
- Research market rates in your area
- Consider total compensation package
- Be transparent about salary ranges
- Highlight non-monetary benefits

### Example Job Posting

```
Position: Senior Chef - North Indian Cuisine
Type: Full-time
Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra

About Us:
Spice Garden is a premium North Indian restaurant chain with 5 locations across Mumbai. We're known for our authentic flavors and exceptional service.

Role Overview:
We're seeking an experienced Senior Chef to lead our kitchen team and maintain our high standards of food quality and presentation.

Key Responsibilities:
• Plan and execute daily menu items
• Supervise kitchen staff and training
• Ensure food safety and hygiene standards
• Manage inventory and cost control
• Develop new dishes and seasonal menus

Requirements:
• 5+ years of professional cooking experience
• Expertise in North Indian cuisine
• Leadership and team management skills
• Food safety certification preferred
• Ability to work in fast-paced environment

What We Offer:
• Competitive salary: ₹35,000 - ₹45,000/month
• Performance bonuses
• Health insurance
• Career growth opportunities
• Meal benefits
• Paid time off

Apply with your resume and portfolio of your culinary work.
```

## Managing Applications

### Application Review Process

1. **Review Applications**: Go to Jobs → [Job Title] → Applications
2. **Filter Candidates**:
   - By application date
   - By experience level
   - By rating/match score
   - By verification status

3. **Evaluate Profiles**:
   - Review resume and experience
   - Check previous employer ratings
   - Assess skill matches
   - Review portfolio/samples

### Application Status Management

#### Status Options
- **New**: Unreviewed applications
- **Under Review**: Currently evaluating
- **Shortlisted**: Potential candidates
- **Interview Scheduled**: Move to interview
- **Rejected**: Not a fit
- **Hired**: Successfully hired

#### Communication Tools
- **Send Messages**: Direct communication with candidates
- **Schedule Interviews**: Built-in scheduling system
- **Share Documents**: Send job details or forms
- **Provide Feedback**: Status updates and next steps

### Interview Management

#### Scheduling Interviews
1. **Select Candidate**: Choose "Schedule Interview"
2. **Choose Format**: In-person, phone, or video
3. **Set Date/Time**: Use built-in calendar
4. **Add Details**: Location, requirements, agenda
5. **Send Invitation**: Automated email to candidate

#### Interview Best Practices
- **Prepare Questions**: Role-specific and behavioral
- **Review Resume**: Know the candidate's background
- **Set Expectations**: Clearly communicate next steps
- **Take Notes**: Document key points and impressions
- **Follow Up**: Provide timely updates

### Making Hiring Decisions

#### Evaluation Criteria
- Skills and experience match
- Cultural fit with team
- Communication abilities
- Availability and flexibility
- References and background checks

#### Extending Job Offers
1. **Select Candidate**: Mark as "Selected for Hire"
2. **Prepare Offer**: Salary, benefits, start date
3. **Send Offer**: Through platform or direct contact
4. **Negotiate**: Handle counter-offers if needed
5. **Finalize**: Get acceptance and prepare onboarding
